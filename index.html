About Me

Hello, my name is Jordon Ng. I am originally from the foggy city of San Francisco in Northern California. After graduating from Abraham Lincoln High School, I moved to Riverside to study at UCR fall of 2010. I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and expected to graduate June 2015.

I am currently a lead lab consultant for the Student Computing Group on campus and a member of the academic and career fraternity Epsilon Iota Sigma. I first started using unix in my freshmen year using CentOS and have since done most of my work on my Windows/Xubuntu dual-boot laptop. Most of my courses have used C/C++ with a touch of some interpretive languages. I'm interested in what the future holds for embedded systems, the IoT, and new forms of gaming.

Outside of class, I enjoy playing video games, hiking, and camping. I have yet to go snowboarding or surfing so there are a lot left or new things for me to try.
